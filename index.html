Organizers: Dr. Zhonghua Zheng, Prof. David Topping, Mr. Junjie Yu

Urban Climate Resilience Workshop

Wednesday 28th May 2025 | Manchester, UK

    Join us for a collaborative workshop focused on enhancing urban climate resilience. This event will bring together researchers, practitioners, and stakeholders to discuss and develop strategies for addressing climate challenges in urban environments. The workshop is funded by UKRI IAA Starter Fund project, in collaboration with Manchester Climate Change Agency and Urban360. We are also looking forward to AI-enabled urban climate modelling/assessment/adaptation.
